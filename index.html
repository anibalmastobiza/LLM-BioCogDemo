Learn More View Demo

Project Overview

Cognitive Science

Investigating how language models replicate human cognitive processes and reasoning patterns in controlled environments.

Bioethics

Evaluating the capacity of AI to engage with complex ethical dilemmas and normative reasoning in biomedical contexts.

Democratic Deliberation

Assessing how language models perform in simulated public discourse and consensus-building scenarios.

Purpose

The primary goal of this project is to understand and evaluate the performance of language models as they apply to complex domains. By assessing their capabilities in cognitive science, ethical reasoning, and public deliberation, we aim to contribute valuable insights into the responsible use of AI.

Explore our methodology

Key Features

01

Rigorous Methodology

Develop a comprehensive framework for assessing AI language models across various domains of human cognition and ethical reasoning.

02

Human-AI Comparison

Explore whether language models can act as proxies for human cognition under specific conditions through direct comparative testing.

03

Best Practices

Establish reliable evaluation frameworks for assessing language models in bioethics, cognitive science, and democratic deliberation.
