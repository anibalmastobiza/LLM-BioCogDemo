Project Overview

This project explores the potential of AI language models to perform tasks in bioethics, cognitive science, and democratic deliberation, assessing their validity and reliability across these critical fields.

Purpose

The primary goal of this project is to understand and evaluate the performance of language models as they apply to complex domains. By assessing their capabilities in cognitive science, ethical reasoning, and public deliberation, we aim to contribute valuable insights into the responsible use of AI.

Key Features

  • Develop a rigorous methodology for assessing AI language models in various domains.
  • Explore whether language models can act as proxies for human cognition under specific conditions.
  • Establish best practices for evaluating language models in bioethics, cognitive science, and democratic deliberation.
