Methodology

The LLM-BioCogDemo project employs an iterative methodology to assess the capabilities of language models across three domains: cognitive science, bioethics, and democratic deliberation. Our methodology combines experimental and qualitative methods to evaluate model performance comprehensively.

Key steps include:

  • Developing task-specific prompts to measure cognitive, ethical, and deliberative abilities.
  • Recruiting a diverse group of human participants for comparative evaluation.
  • Analyzing the data to determine differences between language model and human performance.
