Meet the interdisciplinary researchers driving the LLM-BioCogDemo project

Our Team

The LLM-BioCogDemo project brings together researchers from cognitive science, bioethics, AI, and democratic theory to evaluate language models across complex domains. Our team's interdisciplinary expertise enables us to develop rigorous evaluation methodologies and interpret findings within their appropriate contexts.
