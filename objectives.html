Objectives

The primary objectives of the LLM-BioCogDemo project are as follows:

  • Develop a rigorous methodology to evaluate language models in the domains of cognitive science, bioethics, and democratic deliberation.
  • Explore whether language models can act as proxies for human cognition under specific conditions.
  • Establish best practices for evaluating language models in these critical areas.
